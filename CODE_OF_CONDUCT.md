# How to Save the World — Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as operators, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in How to Save the World a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful** — Treat all participants with respect and dignity
- **Be Constructive** — Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- **Be Collaborative** — Work together toward shared goals
- **Be Patient** — Help newcomers learn and grow
- **Be Inclusive** — Use welcoming, inclusive language
- **Be Transparent** — Communicate openly and honestly
- **Be Accountable** — Take responsibility for your actions

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment** — Intimidation, stalking, unwanted attention
- **Discrimination** — Prejudice based on identity or background
- **Trolling** — Deliberate provocation or disruption
- **Insults** — Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- **Spam** — Unsolicited promotion or advertising
- **Doxxing** — Publishing private information without consent
- **Sexual Harassment** — Unwelcome sexual attention or advances

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repository interactions (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Community forums and chat channels
- Social media interactions about How to Save the World
- In-person events (if any)
- Public spaces when representing How to Save the World

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

**Email:** kb15us@gmail.com  
**Subject:** "Code of Conduct Violation Report"

### What to Include:

- Description of the incident
- Date and time (if applicable)
- Screenshots or evidence
- Names of involved parties (if known)
- Any witnesses
- Your preferred resolution (if any)

All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** — Private written warning explaining the violation
2. **Temporary Ban** — Suspension from community spaces (7-30 days)
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal from all community spaces
4. **Legal Action** — For criminal violations (doxxing, threats, etc.)

The severity of the action depends on:
- Severity of the violation
- Intent (accidental vs. deliberate)
- History of violations
- Impact on the community

---

## Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was unfair:

**Email:** kb15us@gmail.com  
**Subject:** "Code of Conduct Appeal"

Include:
- Your explanation of the incident
- Why you believe the action was unfair
- Any additional context

Appeals will be reviewed within 7 days.

## Community Guidelines

### For Contributors:

- Review pull requests with kindness and clarity
- Welcome newcomers and answer questions patiently
- Assume good intent in disagreements
- Provide context and reasoning for decisions
- Acknowledge when you don't know something

### For Maintainers:

- Respond to issues and PRs in a timely manner
- Explain decisions clearly and respectfully
- Be open to feedback and suggestions
- Treat all contributors fairly
- Uphold this Code of Conduct consistently

### For All Participants:

- Read and understand project documentation before asking questions
- Search for existing issues before creating new ones
- Provide clear, reproducible bug reports
- Be patient with response times
- Celebrate others' contributions
