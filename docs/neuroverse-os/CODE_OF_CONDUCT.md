# NeuroVerse OS — Code of Conduct

## Professional Standards for Licensees and Partners

NeuroVerse OS is proprietary software built on principles of sovereignty, integrity, and respect. All licensees, partners, and collaborators are expected to uphold professional standards of conduct.

---

## 🌟 Our Standards

### We Expect:

- **Respect for intellectual property** — Honor the proprietary nature of NeuroVerse OS
- **Professional communication** — Engage with the Creator and other licensees respectfully
- **Transparency** — Be honest about your use case and deployment scale
- **Compliance** — Adhere to all license terms and conditions
- **Constructive feedback** — Provide actionable, respectful feedback when requested
- **Security consciousness** — Report vulnerabilities responsibly

### We Do Not Tolerate:

- **IP theft or reverse engineering** — Attempting to clone or extract NeuroVerse OS source code
- **License violations** — Using NeuroVerse OS without a valid commercial license
- **Harassment** — Any form of harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior
- **Misrepresentation** — Falsely claiming NeuroVerse OS is open-source or unprotected
- **Unauthorized distribution** — Sharing NeuroVerse OS code or binaries without authorization
- **Disrespectful communication** — Rude, aggressive, or unprofessional conduct

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Warning** — First-time minor violations
- **License suspension** — Repeated or serious violations
- **License termination** — Severe violations (IP theft, harassment, fraud)
- **Legal action** — Criminal violations or egregious IP infringement

The Creator reserves the right to terminate licenses and pursue legal remedies for violations.
